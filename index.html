The Elysium Playbook

We have a systematic framework that we've built over 15 years—across markets, cycles, and continents. It's not magic; it's just math and discipline executed relentlessly.

01

Free Cash Flow Machines

We only buy businesses generating or capable of generating 20%+ cash-on-cash returns. If we can't explain exactly where and how the cash is generated, we don't touch it. No exceptions. No stories. No hypothetical TAMs.

02

Five-Factor Framework

Every investment passes our framework: 1) Cash flow durability, 2) Pricing power, 3) Regulatory moats, 4) Unit economics that improve with scale, and 5) Leadership with skin in the game. Most opportunities fail at least two factors—that's why we pass on 97% of what we see.

03

Asymmetric Capital Deployment

We're not indexers. When we have high conviction, we deploy capital aggressively—sometimes 15-20% in a single position. This concentration has driven our outperformance. When the market gives us outlier opportunities, we take outlier positions.
